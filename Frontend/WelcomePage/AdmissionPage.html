Eligibility Criteria

Admission Process Shishya BEML Public School teaches students the competencies to become higher learners and employable citizens. We strive to not only teach our students but to drive them towards academic excellence. Parents wishing to apply to Shishya BEML Public School should complete the following steps: Arrange a visit to the School by setting an appointment with the admissions office. Request for registration form either from the School office or click on "Enroll now" to fill up the form. Submit filled form along with necessary attachments as mentioned below

ELIGIBILITY • Age limit for Nursery is 3 years as on June 1st of the year of admission. • Age limit for PREP I (LKG) is 3 years and 8 months as on June 1st of the year of admission. • Age limit for PREP II (UKG) is 4years and 8 months as on June 1st of the year of admission. • Age limit for Class I is 5 years and 8 months as on June 1st of the year of admission.

SCHOOL TIMINGS Nursery: Monday to Friday - 08.20am to 12:00pm Prep I to Prep II: Monday to Friday – 8:20am to 12:20pm Nursery to Grade V: Holiday on all saturdays Grade I to VIII: Monday to Friday – 8:20am to 3:10pm, Saturday - 8:20 am to 12:30pm Grade IX to XII: Monday to Friday – 8:10am to 3:10pm, Saturday - 8:10 am to 12:30pm Grade VI to XII: 2nd and 4th Saturday – Holiday

DOCUMENTS FOR SUBMISSION • Filled up Registration Form • Copy of birth certificate • Aadhaar copies • Transfer certificate, progress sheet of the last examination taken • Official copy of reports/marks card from previous school • Four passport size photographs
